FAQS

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for 'non-fungible token.' An NFT is basically data that is accounted for in a digital ledger, and that data represents something specific and unique. An NFT can, for example, represent a unique piece of art or a game token.

When is the launch?

The first release of Project E will take place Dec 17th for Pre-Sale & Dec 20th for Public Sale.

How much will they cost?

Each Project E NFT will cost 0.06 ETH + Gas.

What wallets are supported for minting?

Metamask is currently the only supported wallet.

How can I get an NFT?

It will be available for purchase on our website through an initial sale. At the time of purchase, a randomly selected NFT will be minted on the blockchain and delivered to your wallet and OpenSea account. Once it is sold out, you can get a Project E car (on OpenSea only).

How can I purchase one?

The easiest way is to install a MetaMask extension in your browser and link your Ethereum wallet to it. Ensure you have enough ETH in your wallet to cover the cost of a NFT plus the associated transaction (gas) fees. Approve the transaction on MetaMask and you are all set. The full Project E inventory will be available to mint on www.theprojecte.io only.

What is the total supply?

Project E’s first fleet of inventory consists of 1000 unique Project E cars.

How many Project E cars can I mint?

Each wallet can mint up to 10 cars.

Where can I view my NFT?

Once minted, you'll just need to login into your OpenSea account to view your NFTs, or you can add your NFT to your MetaMask wallet after purchase.
